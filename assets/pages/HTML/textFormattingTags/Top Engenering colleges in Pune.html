1.College of Engineering Pune (COEP)

Established in the era of British in the year 1854, well before any of the formal degree awarding universities took roots in India, College of Engineering Pune (COEP) now known as COEP Technological University (COEP Tech) is the third oldest engineering college in the country, and indeed in Asia

2.Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT)

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) is a private university in Vellore, Tamil Nadu, established in 1984 as Vellore Engineering College and granted deemed university status in 2001. It's known for its strong placement record, diverse campus life, and international collaborations. VIT offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs across various disciplines.

3.MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU)

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) is a private university located in Pune, Maharashtra, India. It's known for its diverse academic programs, including engineering, management, design, and more. Established in 1983, it's part of the MAEER's MIT Group of Institutions and emphasizes both academic excellence and the philosophy of peace studies.

4.All india Shri Shivaji Memorial Society's(AISSM)

AISSMS COE PUNE is an ideal place to gain an engineering education. The futuristic education curriculum and advanced laboratories are changing the way engineering used to be taught. The students of AISSMS COE receive industry-ready education and have always proved their workability in every industry.

5.Dr. D. Y. Patil Institute of Technology

Dr. D. Y. Patil Institute of Technology (DIT), Pimpri, Pune, is a private engineering college affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University. It's known for its focus on empowerment through knowledge and offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs. The institute is located in the heart of Pune and is easily accessible by public transportation.

6.Symbiosis Institute of Technology

Symbiosis also promotes ethical and value-based learning, which is critical for creating responsible and accountable citizens. The department believes that nurturing creativity and encouraging entrepreneurship is another essential aspect of creating a better world.

7.Internationalinstitute of information technology

International Institute of Information Technology, Pune (I²IT), and International Institute of Information Technology, Bangalore (IIITB). I²IT Pune is a private institute affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University, offering undergraduate engineering programs. IIIT Bangalore is a deemed university focusing on education, research, and innovation in IT.

8.Army Institute of Technology (AIT)

The Army Institute of Technology (AIT) Pune offers undergraduate (B.E./B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.E./M.Tech) programs in engineering. Specifically, the B.E./B.Tech programs include specializations in Computer Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, Information Technology, and Mechanical Engineering. AIT also offers an M.E./M.Tech in Data Science.

9.Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University College of Engineering (BVUCOE)

Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University College of Engineering (BVUCOE) Pune is a private engineering college established in 1983 and is affiliated with Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering. The college is known for its research-intensive learning environment and state-of-the-art infrastructure

10.G. H. Raisoni College of Engineering

G H Raisoni College of Engineering (GHRCE) is a private, autonomous engineering institution located in Nagpur, Maharashtra. It is affiliated with Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University and is known for its strong performance in NIRF rankings, particularly in the Innovation category. The college was established in 1996 and is part of the Raisoni Group of Institutions.
