Delecious Veg Biryani Recipe

This Veg Biryani is a flavorfuland easy to make dish that bring joy to every meal.Perfect for weekends ,It's rich in protien and vitamin b12, and test best when served hot.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Banaspati Rice
  • 1 Cup mixed rice
  • Onion(sliced)
  • 1 tsp Biryani Masala
  • Salt,Oil, Mint, Coriender

How To Cook

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes
  2. Saute Onion and Vegetable
  3. Add Spices and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Mixed with cooked rice and simmer
  5. Served hot with raita
