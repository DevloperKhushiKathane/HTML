Healthy and Delicious Recipe

  1. Simple Lentil Soup
    • Step1:Saute 1 chopped onion, 2 carrots, and 2 celery stalks in a pot
    • Step2:Add 1 cup of rinsed brown or green lentils, and 4 cups of vegetable broth.
    • Step3:Stir in 1/2 teaspoon each of cumin and coriander.
    • Step4:Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes.
    • Step5:Season with salt and pepper, and serve hot.
  2. Black Bean and Corn Salsa
    • Step1:Rinse and drain one can of black beans and one can of corn.
    • Step2:Dice half of a red onion and one bell pepper.
    • Step3:Combine beans, corn, onion, and bell pepper in a bowl.
    • Step4:Add the juice of one lime and a pinch of salt.
    • Step5:Mix everything together and serve with whole-grain crackers or as a topping.
  3. Roasted Vegetable Medley
    • Step1:Chop a variety of vegetables like broccoli, bell peppers, and zucchini.
    • Step2:Toss the chopped vegetables with 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
    • Step3:Spread them in a single layer on a baking sheet
    • Step4:Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
    • Step5:Roast at 425°F (220°C) for 20-25 minutes, until tender and slightly caramelized.
  4. Greek Yogurt Parfait
    • Step1:Spoon 1/2 cup of plain Greek yogurt into a glass or bowl.
    • Step2:Top with a handful of fresh berries like blueberries or strawberries.
    • Step3:Add a sprinkle of your favorite nuts, like almonds or walnuts
    • Step4:Drizzle with a teaspoon of honey or maple syrup if desired.
    • Step5:Repeat layers for a larger portion, and enjoy immediately.
  5. Simple Oatmeal
    • Step1:Combine 1/2 cup of rolled oats with 1 cup of water or milk.
    • Step2:Bring to a boil in a small saucepan
    • Step3:Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    • Step4:Remove from heat and let sit for a minute to thicken.
    • Step5:Top with fruit, nuts, or a drizzle of honey.
